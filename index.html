SAT

The is the most popular standardized test for college admissions in the US. It consists of both reading and math multiple choice questions. We offer both timed and untimed versions of the SAT, along with seperate reading and math tests.

ACT

The American College Testing is a standarized test used for college admissions in the US. The test consists of four parts, English, reading, math, and science. We offer both timed and untimed versions of the ACT, along with seperate English, reading, math, and science tests.

GED

The General Educational Development test is a certification that has the equivalency of a high school diploma.The tests consists of 4 subjects that are broken down into 4 seperate tests. The tests are broken down to reading, math, science, and social studies.

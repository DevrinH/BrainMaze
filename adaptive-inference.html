Understanding Inference Questions

Inference questions require you to understand implied meanings rather than directly stated facts.

Example Question

Passage: Emma hesitated before stepping into the grand ballroom...

Which choice best describes Emma’s feelings?

  • A) Overwhelmed by excitement
  • B) Feels out of place
  • C) Intimidated and decides to leave
  • D) Confident and eager to impress

Correct Answer: B) Feels out of place

Explanation: The passage describes Emma’s hesitation and feelings of being an “outsider.”

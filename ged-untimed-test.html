GED Timed Prep Test

This is a timed GED preparation test. The Reasoning Through Language Arts (RLA) section is 150 minutes with approximately 50 questions, Mathematical Reasoning is 115 minutes with approximately 46 questions, Science is 90 minutes with approximately 34 questions, and Social Studies is 70 minutes with approximately 35 questions. Questions are chosen randomly, varying in difficulty, but each test maintains consistent difficulty allocation. There are breaks between sections. Good luck!
